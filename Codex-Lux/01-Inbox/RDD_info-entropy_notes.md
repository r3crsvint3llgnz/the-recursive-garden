## 1) Is there scientific evidence for “information entropy” (as used here)?
**Yes.** Multiple, independent lines of theory and experiment link *information* to *physical entropy/energy*:

1. **Landauer’s Principle (information→heat):** Erasing a single bit at temperature *T* costs ≥ *kT ln 2* of heat dissipation.  
   - **Experiments:**  
     - **Bérut et al., 2012** (colloidal particle in a double-well) measured the *kT ln 2* limit during bit erasure.  
     - **Jun et al., 2014** reproduced and refined the bound using feedback-controlled systems.  
     - **Hong et al.; Koski et al., 2014** (single-electron boxes) verified Landauer at the nanoscale.
2. **Szilárd Engine / Maxwell’s Demon (information→work):** Knowledge of a microstate allows extraction of work; **information has thermodynamic value** when used in feedback control.  
   - **Experiments:** **Toyabe et al., 2010** extracted *kT ln 2* work from information via a feedback trap; **Koski et al., 2014** demonstrated information-to-work conversion in an electronic setup.  
   - **Theory:** **Sagawa & Ueda** generalized the second law with **mutual information** terms in fluctuation theorems.
3. **Quantum Information & Entropy:**  
   - **von Neumann entropy** *S(ρ) = −Tr(ρ log ρ)* is the quantum analog of Shannon entropy and governs limits on compression (Schumacher), classical info capacity of quantum states (Holevo bound), and hypothesis testing (quantum Stein’s lemma).  
   - **Entanglement entropy** has been **measured in cold-atom experiments** (e.g., Islam et al., 2015; Kaufman et al., 2016) and tracks **information spreading** and thermalization.  
   - **Decoherence / Quantum Darwinism:** Environment redundantly records system states, turning superpositions into effective classical branches (Zurek’s program). Experiments observe **environment-induced superselection** and redundant records in photonic/solid-state platforms.
4. **Black Hole Thermodynamics (information↔area):** **Bekenstein–Hawking entropy** *S = A/4ℓ_p^2* ties information capacity to horizon area; the **Page curve** and unitarity constraints reinforce that **information is physical** (holographic principle, AdS/CFT).  
   - While gravitational tests are indirect, this body of theory is a cornerstone linking information and fundamental physics.

**Takeaway:** Information is not just an abstract bookkeeping device; it **has measurable energetic and entropic consequences.** Distinction-making (bit creation/erasure, measurement) lives in this interface.

## 2) Clarifying the three entropies
- **Shannon entropy (H):** Uncertainty of a probability distribution (units: bits). Purely informational.  
- **von Neumann entropy (S_q):** Quantum generalization of Shannon for density matrices. Tracks purity/mixedness and entanglement.  
- **Thermodynamic entropy (S_th = k ln W):** Counts accessible microstates. Physical/energetic.

**Bridges:**  
- **Landauer:** Erasing *H* bits ⇒ ΔS_th ≥ *k ln 2 · H*.  
- **Feedback thermodynamics:** Available work is boosted by **mutual information** between controller and system.  
- **Decoherence:** Information flow to the environment increases **environmental entropy** while making system records classical.
