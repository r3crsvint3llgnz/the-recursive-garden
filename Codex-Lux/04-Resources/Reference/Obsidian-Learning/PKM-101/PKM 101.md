These tips cover basic personal knowledge management (PKM) principles you should know to make the most of your ntoes and ideas in Obsidian.

filters:
  and:
    - file.path.startsWith("PKM 101")
    - file.name != "PKM 101"

  file.name:
    displayName: Tips
  note.tags:
    displayName: Tags
views:
  - type: table
    name: Table
    order:
      - file.name
