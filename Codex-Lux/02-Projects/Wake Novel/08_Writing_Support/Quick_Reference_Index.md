## AI Assistant Usage Notes

### Quick Question Formats
- "Continuity check: [specific detail]"
- "Character reminder: [name]'s current state"
- "Module position: What beat am I hitting?"
- "Timeline: How much time since [event]?"
- "Voice check: How would [character] say this?"

### What AI Should Track
- Plot consistency and character development
- Module scene progression and beat completion
- Timeline and relationship evolution
- Technical detail consistency
- Voice pattern maintenance

### What AI Should NOT Do
- Suggest specific dialogue or prose
- Make creative decisions about plot direction
- Override author's creative instincts
- Write any actual story content
- Judge creative choices (only flag consistency issues)
