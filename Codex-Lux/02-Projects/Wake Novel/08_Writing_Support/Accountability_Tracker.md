## Momentum Tracking

### Positive Momentum Indicators
- [ ] Writing consistently (X days per week)
- [ ] Meeting daily word count goals
- [ ] Excited about upcoming scenes
- [ ] Characters feeling authentic
- [ ] Plot progression feels natural
- [ ] Problem-solving going smoothly
- [ ] Enjoying the writing process

### Warning Signs to Watch For
- [ ] Missing writing sessions frequently
- [ ] Struggling to meet word count goals
- [ ] Avoiding certain scenes or chapters
- [ ] Characters feeling forced or flat
- [ ] Plot feeling contrived
- [ ] Constant second-guessing decisions
- [ ] Losing enthusiasm for the project

### Momentum Recovery Strategies
**When feeling stuck:**
1. Review character motivations and goals
2. Revisit the module beat for current scene
3. Take a step back and outline the next few scenes
4. Work on dialogue or character voice exercises
5. Read favorite authors for inspiration
6. Take a planned break (1-2 days max)
