## Emergency Breakthrough Techniques

### When Nothing Else Works

**Quick Reset Options:**
1. **Write the scene from a different character's POV**
2. **Start the scene later or earlier in the timeline**
3. **Write the worst possible version first, then improve it**
4. **Focus on just the dialogue, add description later**
5. **Write what the characters are feeling, not what they're doing**
6. **Skip this scene and write what happens after**

**Inspiration Triggers:**
1. **Read a favorite author's similar scene**
2. **Watch a movie scene with similar dynamics**
3. **Listen to music that fits the mood you want**
4. **Take a walk and imagine the scene playing out**
5. **Talk through the problem with someone (or AI)**
6. **Write stream-of-consciousness about the characters**

**Perspective Breakers:**
1. **What would happen if you made this scene completely different?**
2. **What if the opposite of your plan happened?**
3. **How would your favorite author handle this?**
4. **What's the most unexpected but logical development?**
5. **What if you combined two planned scenes into one?**
6. **What if you split one scene into two?**

*Remember: Every writer gets stuck. The key is having systematic approaches to work through problems rather than giving up. Use AI as your thinking partner - ask questions, check continuity, and explore options, but trust your creative instincts for the final decisions.*
