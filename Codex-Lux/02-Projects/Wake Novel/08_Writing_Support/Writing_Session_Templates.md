# Writing Session Templates

## Pre-Writing Session Prep (5 minutes)

### Daily Check-In Questions
**Answer these before opening your draft:**

1. **Module Position Check**
   - What chapter am I writing today? ____
   - Which 40-module scenes does this chapter cover? ____
   - What are the key beats that need to happen? ____

2. **Character Arc Status**
   - Whose POV is this chapter? ____
   - Where is this character in their development arc? ____
   - What internal/external change should occur this chapter? ____

3. **Story Continuity**
   - What happened in the last chapter I wrote? ____
   - What unresolved threads need addressing? ____
   - Any technical/world-building details to remember? ____

4. **Session Goal Setting**
   - Word count target for today: ____
   - Main scene objective: ____
   - Emotional tone I'm aiming for: ____

### Quick Reference Checklist
- [ ] Character voice notes reviewed
- [ ] Current timeline position confirmed
- [ ] Key relationship dynamics refreshed
- [ ] Technical details (if relevant) checked
- [ ] Previous chapter ending reviewed

## During Writing Session

### Quick Reference Prompts
**Use these for instant AI assistance without breaking flow:**

- "Remind me: [Character name]'s current emotional state"
- "What did I establish about [technical detail]?"
- "Timeline check: How much time has passed since [event]?"
- "Continuity: Did [character] already know about [information]?"
- "Voice check: How would [character] say this differently?"

### Flow Maintenance
- **Stay in creative mode** - note questions for later rather than stopping to research
- **Trust your instincts** - AI is for support, not creative decisions
- **Capture inspiration** - jot down ideas that come up for future scenes

## Monthly Milestone Review

### Structural Assessment
- **Act progression**: Where am I in the three-act structure?
- **Module compliance**: Are all beats being hit naturally?
- **Character arc completion**: Which arcs are complete/in progress?
- **World-building depth**: What needs more development?

### Writing Process Evaluation
- **Daily writing rhythm**: What's working/not working?
- **AI assistance effectiveness**: How can support be improved?
- **Reference system usage**: What's helpful/unnecessary?
- **Motivation and momentum**: What maintains/drains energy?

### Goal Adjustment
- **Timeline realistic?**: Adjust deadlines if needed
- **Quality standards**: Maintaining/need to adjust?
- **Support systems**: What additional help is needed?
- **Celebration planning**: How to reward major milestones?

## Problem-Solving Session Template

### When You're Stuck

**Identify the Issue:**
- [ ] Don't know what happens next (plot problem)
- [ ] Character feels flat/inconsistent (character problem)
- [ ] Dialogue sounds wrong (voice problem)
- [ ] Scene drags/lacks tension (pacing problem)
- [ ] Details don't match earlier work (continuity problem)
- [ ] Lost motivation/enthusiasm (process problem)

**Structured Problem-Solving:**
1. **Describe the specific issue**: ____
2. **What was working before this point?**: ____
3. **What outcome do I want from this scene/chapter?**: ____
4. **What would [character] actually do here?**: ____
5. **How does this serve the larger story?**: ____

**Solution Brainstorming:**
- Option A: ____
- Option B: ____
- Option C: ____
- **Best choice because**: ____

**AI Assistance Requests:**
- "Help me check continuity for [specific detail]"
- "Remind me of [character]'s motivations and goals"
- "What module beat am I supposed to hit here?"
- "Review my established world-building rules about [topic]"
