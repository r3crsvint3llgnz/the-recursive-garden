## AI Assistant Capabilities

### What AI Can Do for You
- **Instant Reference**: "What did I establish about [detail]?"
- **Continuity Checking**: "Does this conflict with earlier decisions?"
- **Progress Tracking**: Automatic updates to accountability systems
- **Problem-Solving Support**: Structured questions to work through blocks
- **Module Guidance**: "What beat am I hitting? What comes next?"
- **Character Voice Reminders**: Speech patterns and personality notes
- **Motivation Support**: Celebrate achievements and maintain momentum

### What AI Will NOT Do
- Write any prose, dialogue, or creative content
- Make plot or character decisions
- Override your creative instincts
- Judge the quality of your writing
- Provide unsolicited creative suggestions
- Act as co-writer or creative partner
