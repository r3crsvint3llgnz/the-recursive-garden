## Chapter Folder Contents

Each chapter folder contains:

### Standard Files
- **Chapter_X_Outline.md**: Detailed scene breakdown and module beat tracking
- **Chapter_X_Draft.md**: The actual chapter text
- **Chapter_X_Notes.md**: Writing process notes, character insights, continuity reminders

### AI Support Integration
- **Module_Beats.md**: Specific 40-module scenes covered in this chapter
- **Character_Focus.md**: Which characters develop and how in this chapter
- **Continuity_Check.md**: Key details established or referenced
- **Voice_Notes.md**: Character speech patterns and dialogue considerations

## Act-Level Organization

### Act I: Setup & Rising Tension (Chapters 1-7)
- **Module Scenes 0-11**: Foundation, character introduction, call to adventure
- **Key Focus**: Character establishment, world-building, mystery introduction
- **AI Support**: Reference checking, character voice establishment, world consistency

### Act II: Crisis & Revelation (Chapters 8-17)
- **Module Scenes 12-30**: Tests, ordeals, midpoint revelations, crisis escalation
- **Key Focus**: Survival horror, major revelations, character testing
- **AI Support**: Timeline tracking, relationship evolution, consistency with established rules

### Act III: Resolution & Transformation (Chapters 18-25)
- **Module Scenes 31-40**: Climax, resolution, character transformation
- **Key Focus**: Final confrontations, character completion, series setup
- **AI Support**: Arc completion tracking, relationship resolution, series continuity

## AI Integration Benefits

### For Daily Writing
- Quick access to relevant chapter information
- Clear module beat tracking for structural support
- Character development notes for voice consistency
- Continuity checking against previous chapters

### For Project Management
- Visual progress through organized chapter structure
- Easy navigation between related scenes and acts
- Clear tracking of module compliance and character arcs
- Systematic approach to revision and improvement

### For Creative Process
- Maintains focus on current chapter while preserving project overview
- Supports both detailed scene planning and flexible creative discovery
- Provides framework for AI assistance without creative interference
- Balances structure with creative freedom

## Usage Guidelines

1. **Create chapter folder** when beginning new chapter
2. **Start with outline** using template and module guidance
3. **Write draft** with minimal AI interruption for flow
4. **Update notes** immediately after writing session
5. **Use AI** for reference, continuity, and problem-solving support
6. **Maintain organization** for easy navigation and reference

This structure supports the AI coaching approach by providing clear organization and reference materials while maintaining complete creative control over the writing process.
