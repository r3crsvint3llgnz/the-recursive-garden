# Chapter 1 - Reluctant Command

## 40-Chapter Module Integration
**Maps to Scenes 0-2**: Unusual Day → Hint of Death → Inciting Incident  
**Core Function**: Establish protagonist's flaw (boredom), want (adventure), and call to action

## Scene Outline

### Opening Scene
**Setting**: Trade vessel bridge approaching 82 Eridani colony  
**POV Character**: Captain (female, rogue-hearted, competent but bored)  
**Purpose**: Show captain's frustration with routine, establish normal world, hint at change

### Scene Breakdown

#### Scene 1: Bridge Frustration (Unusual Day)
**Length**: ~500-800 words  
**Goal**: Establish protagonist's flaw and ordinary world

*Scene description and key beats:*
- Captain on bridge handling routine trade vessel operations
- Internal monologue showing boredom with bureaucratic missions
- Interaction with AI showing competence but lack of engagement
- Visual details of post-scarcity ship technology and crew efficiency

#### Scene 2: Contract Notification (Hint of Death)
**Length**: ~500-800 words  
**Goal**: Introduce the opportunity that will change everything

*Scene description and key beats:*
- Communication from Earth about new science contract opportunity
- Captain's initial dismissal followed by growing interest
- AI's analytical response vs. Captain's emotional reaction
- Subtle foreshadowing of dangers ahead (wake corridor risks, unknown variables)

#### Scene 3: Decision Point (Inciting Incident)
**Length**: ~500-800 words  
**Goal**: Protagonist accepts call to adventure

*Scene description and key beats:*
- Captain weighs pros and cons of accepting mission
- Conversation with crew revealing character dynamics
- Final decision to accept driven by need for meaning
- Course change toward 82 Eridani colony

## Character Introductions

### Captain (Protagonist)
- **First Impression**: Competent but restless leader going through motions
- **Key Trait Shown**: Rogue-hearted spirit constrained by routine duties
- **Role in Scene**: Demonstrate flaw (boredom) and want (meaningful adventure)
- **Flaw**: Disconnection from purpose, craves excitement over responsibility

### Ship's AI (Mentor Figure)
- **First Impression**: Logical but showing signs of curiosity beyond programming
- **Key Trait Shown**: Analytical capabilities with emerging emotional understanding
- **Role in Scene**: Provide exposition, contrast with Captain's emotional approach
- **Development Arc**: Will become true mentor through consciousness evolution

### Chief Engineer (Crew Representative)
- **First Impression**: Pragmatic, duty-focused, slightly suspicious
- **Key Trait Shown**: Ex-military discipline and systems expertise
- **Role in Scene**: Voice practical concerns, show crew dynamics
- **Function**: Represents crew's perspective on Captain's decisions
