## Core Timeline Established

### Story Setting: Mid-2200s (2250-2260 CE)

#### Historical Progression
- **2040s-2070s**: AGI Achievement Era
  - AGI helps humanity overthrow technocratic/fascist rule
  - Energy abundance through fusion and automated production
  - Full Automation of Labor (FAoL) achieved by 2070s

- **2100s-2130s**: ASI and Solar System Expansion
  - ASI emergence enables von Neumann probe deployment
  - Solar system habitats created for human settlement
  - 2130s: Advanced miniaturized LIGO detectors first detect alien vessels

- **2140s-2160s**: Interstellar Technology Development
  - Wake-following ships developed with ASI-calculated insertion vectors
  - First successful missions following alien vessel gravitational wakes
  - Breakthrough in near-light-speed travel capabilities

- **2170s-2200s**: Multi-System Human Settlement
  - Successful colonization of Alpha Centauri, Proxima Centauri, 82 Eridani
  - Establishment of trade routes and cultural exchange networks
  - Wake corridor navigation becomes routine technology

- **2250s**: Story Present - Mature Interstellar Civilization
  - 80+ years of established interstellar human presence
  - Regular trade and exploration missions between systems
  - Post-scarcity society with extended human lifespans (200-300+ years)

### Miniaturized LIGO Technology
- **Function**: Advanced gravitational wave detectors capable of sensing near-light-speed vessels
- **Development**: Evolved from current LIGO systems through quantum computing advances
- **Story Role**: Enables detection of alien vessels and wake corridor navigation
- **Plot Significance**: Discovery of new wake corridor serves as inciting incident

### Wake Corridor Navigation
- **Technology**: ASI-calculated insertion vectors for following gravitational wakes
- **Development Timeline**: 2140s-2160s research and first successful missions
- **Current Status**: Mature technology enabling routine interstellar travel
- **Story Function**: Both enables journey and creates crisis through corridor instability

### Post-Scarcity Society
- **Foundation**: Energy abundance through fusion and full automation
- **Timeline**: Achieved by 2070s, matured over 180+ years
- **Characteristics**: Material needs met without currency, focus on exploration and self-actualization
- **Character Impact**: Shapes motivations and cultural conflicts in story

- **Technology**: Advanced medical, genetic, and nanotechnology
- **Typical Lifespan**: 200-300+ years with maintained health and vitality
- **Relativistic Effects**: Time dilation for frequent space travelers affects aging
- **Character Implications**: Captain's experience and cultural perspective shaped by extended life
