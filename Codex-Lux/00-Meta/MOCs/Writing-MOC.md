## Writing Agents

Specialized agents for novel writing (see `~/Projects/tools/obsidian-agents/`):

### Core Agents

1. **Continuity Guardian** - Monitors consistency across scenes
2. **Scene Scaffolding** - Generates structured scene templates
3. **Character Voice Analyzer** - Ensures distinct character voices
4. **Timeline Reconciliation** - Maintains chronological integrity
5. **Pacing Velocity** - Analyzes narrative rhythm

### Support Agents

1. **Research Integration** - Connects narrative to factual sources
1. **Worldbuilding Inference** - Extrapolates setting details
1. **Emotional Arc Tracker** - Maps character emotional journeys
1. **Revision Orchestrator** - Manages multi-pass editing
1. **Draft Quality Gatekeeper** - Pre-commit validation
