# Decision filter (pros/cons + framework; no advice)

> [!info]+ Prompt — quick copy
---

I’m choosing between {A} and {B}. 
Give top 3 pros/cons for each, then a decision framework (criteria + weights). 
Do NOT choose for me—show how to decide.
