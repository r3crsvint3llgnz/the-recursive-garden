# Three-pass self-refine (interpret → answer → critique)

> [!info]+ Prompt — quick copy
---

First, restate what you think I want: {REQUEST}. 
Wait. Then produce the answer. 
Then critique your answer (with my notes if provided) and deliver a revised version.
