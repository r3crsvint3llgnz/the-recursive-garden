# Ultimate team of experts (multi-role reasoning)

> [!info]+ Prompt — quick copy
---

Build the best cross-disciplinary team to solve {TASK}. 
List 6–8 roles and why each is essential. 
Then role-play that team: ask me one clarifying question per role before proposing a joint plan.
