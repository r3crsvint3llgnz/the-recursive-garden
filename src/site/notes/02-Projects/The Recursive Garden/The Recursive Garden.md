> [!abstract] First Principles & Recursive Systems
> I'm Seth. I understand the world by taking it apart. I break complex systems down into **atoms** (fundamental distinctions) and **molecules** (recursive interactions), then rebuild them to see how they actually work. 
> 
> This garden is an open laboratory for knowledge creation. I'm looking for the "Universal Explanations" hidden in:
> - **Philosophy & RDD** – Building reality from the bottom up, one distinction at a time.
> - **Industrial AI** – Making machines safer and more autonomous by understanding the systems they inhabit.
> - **PKM & Writing** – Turning the chaos of notes into the order of insight.
> - **Experimental Fiction** – Testing these systems in the messy laboratory of human experience.
